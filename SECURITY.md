## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please write to us at ```q3lkf0xa [at] duck [dot] com``` with the following details:

- A brief description of the vulnerability 
- Steps to reproduce the issue (if applicable)
- Potential impact of the issue

### What to expect:

- We will acknowledge your email within a reasonable timeframe.
- We will send an initial response to your report within some time of submission, however, the time frame can vary depending on the severity of the issue.
- We will probably keep you informed about the progress towards resolving the issue.
- We will announce the issue after it's fixed and give credit where it's due.

Publicly disclosing the vulnerability without giving us ample time to fix it will be considered irresponsible and will be discouraged.

Thank you for helping us ensure the security of our project :)
